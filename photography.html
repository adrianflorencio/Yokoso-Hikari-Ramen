Street Photography

⭐ Daily Life of SMMC Students

Reflection Questions

1. What story does your photo tell?
It tells the story of SMMC students' daily life.

2. Why did you choose this subject?
I chose this subject because as a fellow SMMC student, I can relate to them. Arriving at school before sunrise and going home at sunset.

3. What elements make your photo effective?
The lighting because it makes my photo much clearer.

4. What challenges did you encounter?
The timing and choosing of subject, and the quality of my cellphone camera.

5. How can you improve your shot next time?
By choosing a better angle and improving the quality of my shots.
