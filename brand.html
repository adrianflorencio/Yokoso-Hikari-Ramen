My Brand Story

This logo was created for my ramen shop, Hikari Ramen, inspired by my interest and admiration for Japanese culture. The minimalist design reflects the brand’s simple and clean identity, using a ramen bowl and chopsticks as the main visual elements, along with contrast in the text to highlight the shop’s name and purpose.

The name “Hikari,” meaning light, represents the idea of a warm and comforting bowl of ramen. My inspiration also comes from slice-of-life anime, where cozy ramen shops are often shown during rainy scenes.

This logo represents my dream of visiting Japan and someday opening my own minimalist and welcoming ramen shop.
