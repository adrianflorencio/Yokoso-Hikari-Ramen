Welcome to hikari, a small and cozy hidden shop filled with delicacies.

This is a place where travelers and adventurers gather to rest, enjoy a warm bowl of ramen together and share stories of their journeys.

The soft glow of lights, the light and strong aroma of broth make them feel at home

Whether you are a royalty, commoner, traveler, or adventurer, there is always a hot bowl of ramen waiting for you.
